strangers at home

Ranz & Mackenzie explore tension between their present experiences and longing memories, making them feel like strangers in their delicate homeland. Captured through the lens of 35mm and medium format black and white photography, this work presents the return of Ranz to the Philippines after 10 years and Mackenzie after 4 years. The exhibition narrates tales of home, tradition, and intimate personal evolutions. After being away for so long, can they really it home?
